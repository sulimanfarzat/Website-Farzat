Affiliated companies:

  • FARZAT FOR DEVELOPMENT CO.LTD

    Commercial Company, founded in 1987.

    Industrial company, founded in 2005, on 200,000 sqm.
    It produces about 25,000 MT of caustic soda, and about 50,000 MT HCL.
    In addition to, other important products like CL2. All from Salt as a primary material.
    The products are being distributed domestically and to international markets (Iraq, Turkey, and Lebanon).
    Location / Head office: ALEPPO - SYRIA.

    Location / Head office: 10th of RAMADAN CITY – EGYPT.

  • FARZAT PLASTIC MANUFACTURES CO.LTD.(MEGAPLAST)

    Joint venture (Syrian – South Korean) industrial company, Founded in 2008.
    It produces all types of plastic products.
    Location/ Head office: HESSIA industrial zone – HOMS – SYRIA.
