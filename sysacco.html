ABOUT

Industrial company, founded in 2005, on 200,000 sqm.
It produces about 25,000 MT of caustic soda, and about 50,000 MT HCL. In addition to, other important products like CL2. All from Salt as a primary material. The products are being distributed domestically and to the international markets such as (Iraq, Turkey, and Lebanon).
Location / Head office: ALEPPO - SYRIA.
