Dr.Eng. Mahmoud FARZAT


Curriculum Vitae


  • Full name: Mahmoud Tlas FARZAT.
  • Father and mother: Suleiman and Fatima.
  • Place and date of birth: Rastan - Homs 06/03/1948.
  • Status: married and have 2 girls, and 1 boy.
  • Diplomas:
    • Primary school: Rastan Schools - 1960.
    • Secondary and High school: Homs Schools - 1968.
    • University degree: Damascus University - 1972.
    • PH. Doctor: Bucharest Politeknik High Institute - 1978.
  • Activities:
    • Dr. Mahmoud FARZAT Prize: to be awarded to the top five students in the secondary and high school.
    • Contributing and financing "AL - MAHMOUD Mosque Complex " Rastan - Syria.
    • Contributing and financing " ENG. ZIAD FARZAT for Islamic Scientific Institute" Rastan – Syria.
    • Contributing and financing " AL- MAHMOUD Orphans House" Rastan – Syria. It can hold about 120 orphans.
    • Contributing to build "Dr. Mahmoud FARZAT Main Clock Square" Rastan – Syria.
    • Contributing to build "Dr. Mahmoud FARZAT Main Clock Square" Telbissa – Syria.
    • Contributing to build "AL-MAHMOUD House for Handicapped Children" Telbissa - Syria.
    • Contributing and financing "Al-MAHMOUD Islamic High School- " ( male and female).
    • Contributing to several charities and social parties in Syria.
  • Courses:
    • Course in industrial safety, UNIDO organization - 1973.
    • Course in international economic affairs, Switzerland - 1982.
    • Course in international commercial affairs, Dubai - 2001.
  • Member of several scientific, social, and charitable communities.
  • Current positions:
    • Chairman of Farzat Group for Development - Syria.

    • Founding member of Cham Bank - Syria.
    • Founding member of Syrian Islamic Insurance Co. - Syria.
    • Founding member of Al Sharq Bank - Syria.
    • Board of director member of Qatar National Bank (QNB) - Syria.
    • Deputy Chairman of Qasioun University - Syria.

    • Chairman of M. F. Trading Est. - Lebanon.
    • Chairman of Farzat for Development Co. - Turkey.
